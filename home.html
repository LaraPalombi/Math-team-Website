LaGuardia High School Math Team
Who should join the LaGuardia Math Team?
Anybody with a positive attitude and an interest in math, and who is willing to attend every club meeting. You do not have to be amazing at math to join!
Where will we compete?
In June, we plan to compete at the Princeton University Mathematics Competition and the American Mathematics Competition 12 . In addition, we will participate in various NYU Math Circles.
Who are our presidents, and who is our advisor?
Our presidents will be Lara Palombi, Rebecca Kanter, and Natalie. Club advisor is TBD.
